When you complete this form, you are giving RSN your (limited) personal data and possibly sensitive information about your mentee. The information will be shared internally and may be shared externally if required by law (for instance, for safeguarding).  We may also share certain information with our funders on an anonymised basis.  We retain feedback forms for 10 years from the last date of RSN contact with your mentee. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for more information about how we keep your personal data safe.
